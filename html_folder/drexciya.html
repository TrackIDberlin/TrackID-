DREXCIYA : BETWEEN MUSIC AND FUTURISM

11 March 2024

Drexciya was an influential electronic music group formed in Detroit in the 1990s. It consisted of James Stinson and Gerald Donald, who created music often associated with the electro and techno genres. Drexciya was also known for its deep, futuristic concepts, notably drawing inspiration from science fiction and aquatic themes. They left a lasting imprint on the global electronic scene.

Here are some of Drexciya’s best-known songs:

“Aquabahn”
“Bubble Metropolis
“Hydro Theory”
“Digital Tsunami”
“The Plankton Organization”
“Black Sea
“Andreaen Sand Dunes
“Under Sea Disturbances
“Wavejumper
“Dehydration”


These tracks are often considered emblematic of Drexciya’s distinctive sound, and have helped forge their reputation in the world of electronic music.
