R GAMBLE : SEARCH OF THE PERFECT LOOP AND THE MOST EPIC MELODY

13 March 2024

R Gamble, DJ, producer and founder of the Lost Soul Enterprises label. Lives in New York. Involved in electronic music and associated with the Italian label Marguerite Records. His contribution to the compilation, “Weaklink”, is described as an exciting electro-body track. It’s good to see artists like him contributing to offer a diversity of musical styles.

In his music, this artist explores the boundaries between mutant Wave, Minimal Synth and EBM, creating a dark, damp atmosphere in search of the perfect loop and the most epic melody.
